FAQ

The questions most people need answered before this makes sense.

What kinds of requests are the best fit?

Anything that removes repetitive work, connects tools you already use, or puts AI to work inside your existing operation. The best requests are specific. "Automate X between Y and Z" ships faster than "help us with AI."

How fast does work get done?

Most requests are completed within 2-5 business days. Larger requests are broken into smaller pieces, with visible progress and updates every 24-48 hours while active.

Do I need the audit first?

For most new clients, yes. It is how I get into the weeds of your business and build a real plan. In straightforward cases, I may waive it and start directly in the membership.

How do calls work?

The membership is async by default. If you want regular strategy calls, they are available as a paid add-on. Most clients do well with async updates and Loom walkthroughs.

What if I only have one or two requests?

That is fine. Many clients use the membership in focused bursts. If you hit a slower stretch, you can finish the current month and come back when you need more.

How do larger requests get handled?

Broken into chunks and shipped piece by piece. You will see progress every couple of days until the request is complete. If something turns into a much larger build, I will tell you and we can scope it separately.

What tools do you work with?

Most modern stacks - Make, n8n, Zapier, OpenAI and Anthropic APIs, Airtable, Notion, Webflow, common CRMs, and most SaaS tools with solid APIs. If your stack is unusual or especially complex, we will talk through whether it belongs in the membership or as a separate project.

What if I am not technical?

You do not need to be. Part of my job is translating between your business and the tech. Loom walkthroughs are included so your team can actually use what gets built.

What is outside the membership?

Big custom builds and ongoing technical leadership usually are not a fit for this format, though some can be handled as separate fixed-scope projects when the scope is clear and the timing works.
