FAQ

The short version before we talk.

What makes a request a good fit?

It should remove repeated work, connect tools you already use, or help the team act faster on information. Specific requests move fastest.

How fast do requests move?

Most active requests move in 2-5 business days. Larger work is broken into smaller pieces with updates every 24-48 hours while active.

Do I need the audit first?

Yes for most new clients. It creates the roadmap and protects both of us from starting a membership around vague priorities.

What is outside the membership?

Large SaaS builds, emergency production support, broad technical leadership, and complex migrations are separate projects if they make sense at all.

What if I am not technical?

That is fine. You bring the business context. I translate the work into tools, automations, prompts, docs, and Loom handoffs your team can use.
